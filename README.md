# Biz Lens Automata

**Biz Lens Automata** is an AI-powered business analytics and automation project designed to support executive and management-level decision making through clear, structured insights.

🌐 **Live Application**  
👉 https://biz-lens-automata.lovable.app

---

## 📌 Project Overview

Organizations generate large amounts of data, yet executives and decision-makers often lack a unified and automated way to transform that data into actionable insights.

Biz Lens Automata addresses this gap by providing a **business-oriented analytical lens** that combines:
- Business Intelligence principles  
- Data analytics workflows  
- Automation and AI-assisted insights  

The project is positioned as a **concept product and working prototype**, bridging business needs with modern analytics capabilities.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

- Provide decision-makers with clear, KPI-driven insights  
- Reduce manual reporting and analysis efforts  
- Enable faster, data-informed decisions  
- Demonstrate how AI and automation can enhance business analytics  
- Serve as a scalable foundation for advanced analytics solutions  

---

## 🧠 Solution Concept

Biz Lens Automata is structured around four core layers:

1. **Data Layer**  
   - Business and operational datasets  
   - Structured analytical inputs  

2. **Analytics Layer**  
   - Data preparation and transformation  
   - Descriptive and diagnostic analysis  

3. **Automation & AI Layer**  
   - Automated insight generation  
   - Rule-based or AI-assisted recommendations  

4. **Presentation Layer**  
   - Executive dashboards  
   - KPI summaries and decision views
